Hey, we want to solve a problem

Our Civil Action Project (CAP) centers around addressing the issue of children's lack of coding knowledge and preparedness for the future. We recognize that in today's world, technology plays an integral role in shaping the future, and we believe that every child should have access to the necessary tools and skills to thrive in this rapidly evolving digital landscape. Through our CAP, we aim to equip children with the coding skills and knowledge they need to succeed and make a positive impact on the world.

About Us We are a group of 8th-grade boys brought together by the supernatural force we call Mr. Mindes. After fighting through the challenge in our group that we call Zimmy, we finally succeeded in making a curriculum that will help future generations(7th graders) for years to come.

Our Problem

To address the problem of children's lack of coding knowledge and preparation for the future, we came up with a solution for our Civil Action Project (CAP). Our plan was to develop a website that offers a 6-week coding curriculum designed specifically for children. Our website is aimed at making coding fun and accessible for kids of all ages, with step-by-step lessons that are easy to follow. Our hope is that by providing this resource, we can empower children with the tools they need to develop their coding skills and pave the way for a brighter future.
